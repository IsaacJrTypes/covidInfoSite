King County Indoor Covid Guidelines

  • Establishments and events included in this order are required to display this signage at all public entrances.
    (Source: King County)

  • People ages 12 and older are required to show proof of full COVID-19 vaccination.
    (Source: King County)

  • Negative test result (within 72hrs) required to enter certain indoor and outdoor events and establishments in King County if not vaccinated.
    (Source: King County)

  • Outdoor dining, grocery stores, and take-out are exempt.
    (Source: Public Health Insider)

What can be used as proof of vaccination?

  • A CDC vaccine card or photo of vaccine card.

  • Documented proof of vaccination from medical record or vaccine provider.

  • Proof of vaccination from another state or county.

  • Printed certificate or digital record from MyIRMobile.com or other apps.
    (Source: King County)
